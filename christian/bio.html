    • Biography

    Wendy Farha is a Christian singer, comedian & speaker. For more than 40 years, she has been delighting audiences with her beautiful songs & hilarious sense of humour.

    Diagnosed with clinical depression in 2000 & then with breast cancer in 2009, Wendy knows what it is to walk through adversity. Now in remission, she tours Canada & the USA extensively, sharing her music, comedy & her touching life story.

    Invite Wendy to your event today & hear how sadness and despair can be turned into hope & JOY!

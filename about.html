    • Biography

    I love making people laugh! Laughter is such a great healer, and we all need to lighten up once in awhile.

    I learned this as a result of being diagnosed with clinical depression in 2000, and then with an aggressive form of breast cancer in 2009. Life is tough....we need to laugh! You can read about how cancer turned me into a comedian right here!

    As a singer-songwriter, I also enjoy sharing my songs with others. Music is a powerful tool that allows us to communicate to the deepest parts of the heart. I feel very grateful that I get to do what I do for a living.

    As a public speaker, motivating people to be all they can be is another passion of mine: helping others to identify their fears, begin to find healing & wholeness, and reach forward & upward to realize their full potential.

    We should never assume we've got tomorrow. I now live every day not as if it's my last, but as if it's my best!
